# Reviewer 2 Computational Notes

## miR-140-3p Prioritization

Reviewer 2 comment 3: miR-140-3p was prioritized because the biologically filtered network connected it to strongly induced CEMIP and predicted circPAPPA. In the final miRNA analysis, miR-140-3p is reduced after both cytokines, nominally significant after IL-4, not nominally significant after IL-13, and not FDR significant. It remains biologically prioritized because CEMIP is strongly induced and FDR significant after both cytokines, miR-140-3p is directionally concordant, the curated integrated network table provides miRanda score and predicted binding energy for miR-140-3p/CEMIP and miR-140-3p/circPAPPA, the axis is fibrosis relevant, and functional assays support the CEMIP candidate relationship.

Recommended wording: "miR-140-3p showed a modest, directionally concordant reduction following IL-4 and IL-13 treatment and was prioritized because of its predicted interaction with strongly induced CEMIP, its relevance to fibrosis, and subsequent functional support."

## Additional Targets

Reviewer 2 comment 1: additional predicted candidate axes were evaluated experimentally, including miR-135b-5p/TNC and miR-625-3p/LIFR. The curated integrated network also links miR-135b-5p to circLIFR. The revised manuscript should distinguish the strongest supportive miR-140-3p/CEMIP result from negative or less supportive results for the additional evaluated candidates and should not imply that all candidate network interactions were validated.

## Figure 5A Labeling

Reviewer 2 comment 4: Figure 5A should explicitly label the miRNA, mRNA, and circRNA identities shown in the schematic. Label miRNA: miR-140-3p; mRNA: CEMIP mRNA; circRNA: circPAPPA / hsa-circ_0002052. The caption should state that circPAPPA was the predicted circRNA partner for the miR-140-3p/CEMIP axis but was not functionally supported if the circPAPPA knockdown remained negative.

## circPAPPA Classification

If circPAPPA functional support is not supplied, classify circPAPPA as a predicted or biologically prioritized component rather than an experimentally supported mechanism. Do not claim that the complete circPAPPA-miR-140-3p-CEMIP competitive mechanism has been validated unless direct supporting data and replicate details are provided. Edges in the network figure should be described as miRanda-predicted interactions, not experimentally proven physical competition.

## No New Experimental Reanalysis

No new experimental replicate analysis was performed in this computational finalization pass. If additional raw validation data are supplied, those data should be analyzed separately with panel-specific biological n, technical-replicate handling, error bars, and statistical tests documented.
